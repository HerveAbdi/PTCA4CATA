Add beautiful convex hulls to your graphs

Dr. Jean Bon

2017-10-20

This is a short example on how you can add beautiful convex hulls around your data in ggplot2 graphs.

library(ggplot2)
library(PTCA4CATA)

One group

set.seed(123)
df <- data.frame(x = rnorm(10), y=rnorm(10))

ggplot(data=df, aes(x, y)) + 
  geom_point() +
  ggConvexHull(df)
